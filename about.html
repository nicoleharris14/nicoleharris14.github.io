About Nicole's Cupcake Shop

Nicole's Cupcake Shop was founded in 2016 by Nicole Harris. She graduated from the School of Cupcake Decorating in Paris, France and decided to come back to her hometown to share what she had learnt.

Nicole's cupcake shop has been recognized as Toronto's most successful small business, with only 5 employees. The shop was also named "Toronto's Best Desert Shop" by BlogTO.

Nicole hopes to one day expand her company so that she can offer her cupcakes in all the provinces and territories of Canada.
